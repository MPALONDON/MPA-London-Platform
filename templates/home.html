Our Story

Music Performance Academy emerged from a shared vision among passionate musicians who believed in the transformative power of collaborative music-making. We set out to create something different from traditional music education.

Our journey has been guided by the belief that music is not just about individual mastery, but about the connections we forge through shared experiences.

While traditional music education often isolates students in individual practice rooms, we've built an environment where musicians learn together, grow together, and create together. Our students don't just learn to play instruments—they learn to listen, respond, and harmonize with others, developing skills that extend far beyond the stage.

Today, we offer a diverse range of memberships designed to nurture both individual talent and collective creativity. Our commitment remains unwavering: to provide an inclusive space where music lovers can discover their voice, find their rhythm, and experience the profound joy of making music with others.

Our Team

At Music Performance Academy, we are privileged to have a team of dedicated musicians whose diverse backgrounds and extensive experience enrich our collaborative music-making environment.

Bello-Pluto Salvatore

Guitar & Bass Instructor

Bello-Pluto Salvatore is a seasoned musician specializing in guitar and bass. Holding a degree in Music Performance, he has been actively involved in the music scene for over a decade. His approach emphasizes building a strong technical foundation while nurturing expressive musicality. Bello-Pluto works with musicians of all ages and skill levels, tailoring sessions to individual interests and goals. Beyond teaching, he continues to perform, bringing real-world experience to our collaborative environment.

Pavels Vasjukovs

Guitar & Bass Instructor

Pavels Vasjukovs brings a wealth of experience as a guitarist and bassist. With years of performance and teaching under his belt, he specializes in various genres, including rock, jazz, and classical. Pavels believes in a collaborative approach, focusing on making sessions both enjoyable and educational. He encourages musicians to explore different styles and develop their unique sound. His performances across Europe have enriched our environment, providing insights into the professional music world.

Peter Krieg

Drummer & Instructor

Peter Krieg is an accomplished drummer with a passion for rhythm and groove. His extensive experience includes writing and performing music across Europe, collaborating with various artists and ensembles. Peter's approach is rooted in developing solid timing and coordination, essential for any drummer. He works with musicians ranging from beginners to advanced players, aiming to instill confidence and proficiency. Peter also offers workshops and masterclasses, sharing his expertise and inspiring the next generation of collaborative musicians.

Together, our musicians create a dynamic collaborative environment, combining technical expertise with a passion for music. We are committed to guiding you on your musical journey, fostering both skill development and a lifelong appreciation for the transformative power of making music with others.
